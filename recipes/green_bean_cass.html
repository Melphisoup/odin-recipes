Green Bean Casserole

Description

This is an easy green bean casserole I make when I'm feeling a little lazy. It's great when the weather get's a little colder out and I make it with cream of chicken and mushroom for a little extra heartyness.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound green beans
  • 2 cans cream of chicken and mushroom soup
  • 12 oz crispy onions

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Cut the green beans to size and blanche for 5 minutes
  3. mix green beans soup and 8 oz of crispy onions in a casserole dish
  4. Bake 20-30 minutes and add remaining crispy onions on top
  5. bake another 5 minutes and remove from oven to cool
