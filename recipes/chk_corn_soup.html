Melph's Chicken Corn Soup

Description

This is my own personal chicken soup recipe I make at home all the time. Since it's getting to be that season again I thought I'd share it!

Ingredients

  • 2 large yellow onions
  • 1 carrot
  • 2-3 celery stalks
  • About 1 piund boneless chicken breast
  • About one pound bone in thighs
  • 2-3 potatoes
  • Pot pie noodles

Steps

  1. Cut the onions,carrots and celery into equal parts.
  2. get them going on a low heat to sweat, avoid browning your mire poix.
  3. when the mire poix is ready, about 20-30 minutes later, start filling the pot with water and get the thighs in.
  4. let the broth cook for a few hours and when it's done strain the liquid into a seperate container
  5. you can save the thigh meat and add that and the broth back to the pot
  6. cut the potatoes and breast meat and add to the broth
  7. add the pot pie noodles last and when they're soft it's done!
